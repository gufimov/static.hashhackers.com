Steps to Create a User Page.

  • Create a account on Wikipedia.
  • Choose a Username like John_Doe instead of John Doe.
  • Fill the spaces with underscores and Every First letter should be Capital (not required but better to be).
  • After you login you will see your name at top of the page. saying user page doesn’t exist.
  • Click on it and Create your User Page.
  • You may get redirected to meta.wikimedia.org site but every user page on this site also reflects on Wikipedia (not available in search).
  • There you can write about yourself and add you photos.
  • Feel free to check my Wikipedia Page and click on edit to check how it was written.
